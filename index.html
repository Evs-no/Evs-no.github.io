NO SIBLING LEFT BEHIND

The siblings of pediatric cancer patients have similar emotions to that of the parents.

SHINE LIGHT ON THE SIBLINGS OF PEDIATRIC CANCER PATIENTS

Relations With the Family

When a family has a child diagnosed with cancer, it not only affects the child and the parents but it also affects the siblings in the family. The brother or sister will have their entire world flipped. They may feel neglected and resentful but at the same time hide those feelings because they don't want the rest of the family to be worried. It is extremely important to connect with the siblings just as much as the parents or the patient.

- How to keep siblings involved -

Talk on the phone, videochat, or play games together on the Internet.
